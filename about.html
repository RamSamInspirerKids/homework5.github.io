    What Are Computers

    Computers are a piece of technology that are used everday for different cenarios like Productivity, Gaming, Education and More. Usually each computer has different puposes, For Example, A high spec and powerful computer is mainly used for Gaming, Modeling, Video/Photo editing, Programing and more.
