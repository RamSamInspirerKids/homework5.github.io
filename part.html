    Parts Required in Computers

    On this page I will walk you through the parts of a computer.

    The First part is the CPU or Central Processing Unit or Proccesor. The cpu is the brains of the computer and does all the tasks. Each Task is assigned the a core, Depending on what cpu you have there will be from 2-64 cores. Each core has one or two threads that do the threads. Usually there are two threads per cpu but there is a chance for one thread per cpu.Image

    The Second Part is the RAM or Random Acess Memory. Ram is used to store information that is temorary like app windows or chrome tabs. Also, RAM is used to send information and retrive infromation from the CPU and send it to other parts. The RAM is measured in gigabyets and have dim's that contain that memory.Usualy, RAM dim can have between 1-32 GigaBytes.Image

    The Next Part is the GPU or Graphics Proccesing Unit or Graphics Card. The GPU powers the pixels on the display. When a user moves their cursor and clicks on the Google Chrome icon the gpu adds the changes to the pixels on the display to show that. Also, GPU's Help in Rendering and Gaming. GPU's come in different sizes and some come built in with the cpu.Image

    The Next Part is the Storage, There are two devices for Storage the SSD which is fast but can not hold too much data and the opposite on the HDD, Instead of writing SSD/HDD to represent both items I will be using SSD. An SSD is the device that holds all the data of your computer. They come in different sizes, shapes and prices. The SSD is measured in GB or TB and the capacity is between 120GB-8TB(A HDD can hold upto 18TB).Image

    The next part is the Motherboard, THe Motherboard is the key to everything as eveything plugs into it. Motherboards can vary between shapes,sizes and prices depending on your CPU,GPU,RAM,SSD/HDD and Case.Image

    The Second Last Part is the PSU or Power Supply. The Power Supply powers all the componets and has the port for the power cable to plug into the wall outlet. The PSU is meausered in watts, Depending on your parts the powersupply wattage might differ as they range from 450-1600 watts retail.Image

    The Last Part is the Case, The case is the part that houses all the componets inside a proctected box. Cases are different sizes, shapes and prices depending on the size of your motherboard, GPU and cooler.Image
